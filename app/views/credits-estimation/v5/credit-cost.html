Credit prices

This service provides an estimate only, based on the current statutory credit prices and the information you provided.

It provides an indication of how much you can expect to pay for statutory biodiversity credits. The actual price for them may be different.

Not legally binding

The estimate is not legally binding with Natural England, or the Secretary of State. Your application to purchase statutory credits is subject to you meeting the required eligibility checks.

Your final statutory credit price, based on your application, will be shown on your completed invoice.

Your responsibility

It is your responsibility to ensure you have purchased the correct number, and tier, of biodiversity credits to meet your BNG requirements.

All prices listed in this service do not include VAT, but VAT is payable.

Read the statutory biodiversity credits guidance for further information.
